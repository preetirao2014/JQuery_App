About us
The Massage Center is open for business on Monday through Saturday between 8 AM and 7 PM. We have many skilled licensed massage therapists available to provide relaxation, rejuvenation and restoration of balance to your body.
We are located in a very convenient location with 24 hr parking. It is easily accessible and situated in a clean and safe locality. The surroundings are very peaceful and quiet to help you relax during the massage.

Most of our therapists have atleast 5 to 8 years experience and a degree in the field. Several of us hold an additional Master Bodyworker certification. We are all easygoing, friendly and focused on the healing aspects of our profession.
